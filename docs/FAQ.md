# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is XANNRZ Portfolio?
XANNRZ Portfolio is a professional portfolio website builder designed for developers, designers, and creative professionals.

### Who can use XANNRZ Portfolio?
Anyone can use XANNRZ Portfolio. It's designed for freelancers, developers, designers, and anyone who wants to showcase their work.

### Is XANNRZ Portfolio free?
Yes, XANNRZ Portfolio is free to use. We also offer premium features for advanced users.

### How do I get started?
Visit our website and sign up for an account. Follow the setup wizard to create your portfolio.

## Technical Questions

### What technologies does XANNRZ Portfolio use?
XANNRZ Portfolio is built with React, Next.js, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS.

### Can I customize my portfolio?
Yes, you can customize colors, fonts, layout, and more through our customization panel.

### Can I use my own domain?
Yes, you can connect your own domain to your XANNRZ Portfolio.

### Is my data secure?
Yes, we use HTTPS encryption, secure authentication, and follow security best practices.

### Can I export my data?
Yes, you can export your portfolio data at any time.

## Support Questions

### How do I contact support?
You can contact us through email, GitHub Discussions, or our Discord community.

### How long does it take to get a response?
We typically respond within 24 hours.

### Can I get a refund?
Since XANNRZ Portfolio is free, there are no refunds. Premium features have a 30-day money-back guarantee.
