# Community Guidelines

## Welcome to XANNRZ Portfolio Community!

We're excited to have you here! This document outlines the guidelines for participating in our community. By joining, you agree to follow these guidelines.

## Our Community Values

### Inclusivity
We welcome people of all backgrounds, experiences, and perspectives. We strive to create an environment where everyone feels valued and respected.

### Collaboration
We believe in working together to solve problems and create amazing things. We encourage sharing ideas, asking questions, and helping each other grow.

### Respect
We treat each other with respect and dignity. We listen to different viewpoints and engage in constructive dialogue.

### Transparency
We believe in being open and honest about our decisions, processes, and challenges. We welcome feedback and are committed to continuous improvement.

### Excellence
We strive for high-quality work and continuous learning. We support each other in achieving our goals and celebrating our successes.

- Ask questions about the project

### Pull Requests
- Contribute code improvements

### Discord/Slack
- Real-time discussions

## Getting Involved

### For Beginners
- Start by reading the documentation
- Join our Discord community
- Ask questions in GitHub Discussions
- Pick a "good first issue" to work on

### For Contributors
- Review open issues and PRs
- Help answer community questions
- Improve documentation
- Submit bug fixes and features

### For Maintainers
- Review and merge PRs
- Respond to issues and discussions
- Guide community discussions
- Make strategic decisions

## Communication Guidelines

### Be Respectful
- Use inclusive language
- Avoid offensive comments
- Respect different opinions
- Listen actively

### Be Clear
- Write clear and concise messages
- Use proper formatting
- Provide context and examples
- Ask clarifying questions

### Be Constructive
- Offer solutions, not just criticism
- Provide actionable feedback
- Acknowledge good work
- Celebrate contributions

### Be Responsive
- Respond to messages in a timely manner
- Acknowledge receipt of feedback
- Follow up on discussions
- Keep conversations on track

## Code of Conduct

All community members must adhere to our Code of Conduct. We do not tolerate:

- Harassment or discrimination
- Offensive language or behavior
- Threats or intimidation
- Doxxing or privacy violations
- Spam or self-promotion

See [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) for more details.

## Reporting Issues

If you witness or experience inappropriate behavior:

1. **Document** the incident with details and timestamps
2. **Report** to the community team at conduct@xannrz.com
3. **Do Not** publicly call out the person (unless necessary for safety)
4. **Allow** the team to investigate and respond

## Community Events

### Weekly Discussions
- Every Monday: Project updates and announcements
- Every Wednesday: Feature discussions
- Every Friday: Community showcase

### Monthly Meetups
- First Thursday: Community hangout
- Third Thursday: Technical deep dive
- Last Thursday: Planning and feedback

### Annual Conference
- Multi-day event with talks, workshops, and networking
- Open to all community members
- Free for contributors

- **Advocates**: Highlighted in community updates
- **Sponsors**: Recognized in project materials
